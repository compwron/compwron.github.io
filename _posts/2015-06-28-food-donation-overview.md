There are several organizations that will come pick up leftover catered food or raw supplies and take them to a nearby distribution shelter. This is frequently called ["food rescue"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Food_rescue)

* [Feed Forward](https://www.feedingforward.com/)
* [Food Runners](http://blog.foodrunners.org/)
* [Food Rescue](http://www.foodrescue.net/)
* [Second Helpings](https://www.secondhelpings.org/)
