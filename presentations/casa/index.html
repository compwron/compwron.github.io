üìù A note on purpose

Be aware that your purpose as a project (may) be more than making a working tool for some users. Our purpose is also to help people learn how to contribute, and I consider it equally important. I could theoretically (for a while at least) build it all myself but that‚Äôs not what I want (time, flexibility, mental health) This may not be you! If you are big/popular project with more contributors than you want & a pack of paid maintainers, then this is not your problem (but turning the contributors that you have *into* the contributors that you *want* can work!) If you want more contributors than you have, you have to consciously and conspicuously invest in making sure that people know what to do. Office hours for people who want a human face, slack reminders for people who want to show up and figure it out, a good readme so people *can* figure it out, issues that explain what needs doing using url&screenshot&login rather than domain terms that new contributors won‚Äôt know
